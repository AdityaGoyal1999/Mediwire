About Us

Our Inspiration

The market growth of global electronic medical record market has been accelerated due to feasibility in data availability across the globe and growing need of integrated healthcare system. According to Reuter Health, "less than one in three U.S. hospitals can find, send, and receive electronic medical records for patients who receive care somewhere else". We are looking to promote efficient data entry, thorough and complete patient data, and uniformity across institutions. Ultimately presenting an enormous capital investment for many hospitals and other institutions throughout the country.

Our Goal

To provide a system to help:

- Health care providers to connect with their patients, easily access and update their EMR (Electronic Medical Records) and add new patients to their personal record database.

- Patients to connect with their doctors and to have quick access to a summary of their medical history, which includes information such as demographics, vital signs, diagnoses, medications, treatment plans, progress notes, problems, immunization dates, allergies, radiology images, and laboratory and test results.

To our Doctors:

All around the world, Doctors are required to hold onto patient files for at least 10 years after the patient’s last visit—If your clinic has been in business for any length of time, you have a lot of files to keep track of. We can help with that problem, our secure database helps you store all of your patients' records in a simple and straightforward way. We all live in a digital age, so why maintain your patients' records in an old-fashion way?

Registration

When you go on the MedWire homepage, you'll be able to sign up as a doctor or a patient on the platform. Doctors will be verified before they are given access to patients' records. Once you have registered, make sure that you use the same credentials you registered with to log in.
